The Future of Coding

For decades, turning an idea into software required learning syntax, frameworks, deployment, and countless details that had nothing to do with the idea itself.

With Cursor, we're finally approaching a world where the distance between ideas and reality becomes zero.

There is great work to be done. And you're part of it.
